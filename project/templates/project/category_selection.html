How to Play Ethiopian Trivia

  1. Click the link in a category to choose which category to play in
  2. Answer the 20 questions in your category and finish the quiz
  3. You get 5 points for each correct answer and it is scored out of 100
  4. View the details of your quiz and see where you stand on the leaderboard

Features of the Ethiopian Trivia App

  • Take a trivia quiz in a category of your choice and learn about Ethiopia
  • Track your trivia score and view your progress
  • View your trivia scores visually with a graph
  • Compete with other users on the leaderboard
  • Earn badges for your trivia attempts

Badges You Can Earn

  • Perfectionist | Scored 100 on a trivia attempt
  • Dedicated | Completed 5+ trivia attempts
  • Veteran | Completed 10+ trivia attempts
  • Versatile | Attempted 3+ different categories
  • Improver | Improved score from first to lastest score
